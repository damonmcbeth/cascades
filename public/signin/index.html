What is CASCADES?

CASCADES is a free easy to use tool that helps you get stuff done. With features the customizable kanban style task board and the team-based collaboration features, CASCADES enables you and your team to track and close your projects.

Task Management

Easily customise CASCADES to suit your start up, portfolio or product. Take advantage of the layered Sketch file and bring your product to life.

Project Tracking

Designed with modern trends and techniques in mind, CASCADES will help your product stand out in an already saturated market.
